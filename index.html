Introduction

Welcome to the professional resume of Lawrence Murry, CF APMP, a results-driven Senior Proposal Professional with over a decade of experience in proposal project management, technical proposal writing, RFP management, proposal content management, and delivering high-impact SaaS solutions for government, nonprofit, and enterprise clients. Specializing in cloud-based SaaS proposal management Lawrence is Shipley trained and APMP certified. Lawrence combines strategy, compliance, and efficiency to craft winning proposals that align with business goals. You will find that his impeccable professional credentials and client testimonials has made a great impact across the technology and the public sector landscape.
